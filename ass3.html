Mouse

Family: Muridae

Reproduction Method: Mice reproduce sexually,
with females going through estrous cycles.
The gestation period is around 19-21 days,
and the female gives birth to a litter of
baby mice known as "mouse pups

Elephant:

Family: Elephantidae

Reproduction Method: Elephants reproduce sexually,
with female elephants coming into estrus and mating
with a male elephant. The gestation period lasts up
to 22 months, and the female gives birth to a calf,
also known as a "baby elephant."

Tiger:

Family: Felidae

Reproduction Method: Tigers reproduce through sexual
mating. After a gestation period of about 93-112 days,
the female gives birth to a litter of tiger cubs. The
mother provides care and protection for the cubs during
their early stages of life.
